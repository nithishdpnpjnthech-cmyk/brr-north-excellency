🏡 BRR North Excellency Phase-1 – Quick FAQs

BRR North Excellency Phase-1 is located in Thanisandra, North Bengaluru, offering excellent connectivity to Manyata Tech Park, Nagawara, Hebbal, and key IT corridors.

The project offers well-designed 2 BHK and 3 BHK apartments, planned for comfortable urban living.

Prices start from ₹1.06 Cr onwards, depending on the configuration, floor, and facing.
(Prices are subject to change as per availability.)

BRR North Excellency Phase-1 is developed on approximately 1.35 acres with around 104 apartments in a residential block.

The project is currently under construction, with possession Starts from July 2027.
(Exact dates can be confirmed with the sales team.)

The project features modern lifestyle amenities including a clubhouse, gym, children’s play area, landscaped spaces, indoor games, and 24/7 security, as per project specifications.

The project has 65 percent open and 35 percent constructed.

BRR North Excellency Phase-1 is developed by BRR HallMark Developers lLLP.
